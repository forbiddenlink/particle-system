Privacy Policy

This policy describes how the Nova Particles demo handles information. The demo is intended for technical exploration and does not require account creation. We do not intentionally collect personal profile data through core simulation controls.

What may be processed

  • Basic request logs generated by hosting infrastructure
  • Browser and device information used for compatibility diagnostics
  • Information voluntarily included in support emails

How information is used

Data is used to keep the service available, diagnose rendering issues, and improve reliability across browsers. We do not sell personal data and do not use the demo as a behavioral advertising platform.

Retention and security

Operational logs are retained only as long as needed for security and debugging. Access is limited to authorized maintainers. If you contact support, retain only the correspondence required to resolve the issue and maintain an auditable support trail.

Requests and updates

To request deletion of support correspondence, contact privacy@nova-particles.dev. Policy updates will be reflected on this page with a revised publication date.
